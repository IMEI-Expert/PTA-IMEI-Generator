    What is an IMEI Number?

    IMEI stands for International Mobile Equipment Identity. It is a unique 15-digit code assigned to every mobile device. In Pakistan, all phones must have a PTA approved IMEI to be registered and used on local networks. The IMEI is crucial for tracking, registering, and verifying your phone’s legality.
